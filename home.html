About us

A firm for the production of the metallic goods, building equipment and international transportation, was founded in 1990 like a result of the activity expansion and the production volumen increase of the former Machine and locksmith shop that had been founded in 1980.

Since the very beginning, the Firm manufactured connecting elements in machinery like bolts, nuts, rivets, washers etc.

As market has been inexorable in making producers to keep searching for greater innovations, “IKAR” d.o.o. also started developing and marketing new programmes like the following:

- Building equipment programmes
- Mechanical sets for the TV signal reception equipment

From the mentioned programmes, the Firm successfully manufactures as follows:

- Telescopic props
- Facade scaffoldings with all the components
- Clamps for scaffoldings
- Other products in connection with building
- Mechanical parts and sets for the TV signal reception
